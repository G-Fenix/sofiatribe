ABOUT US

A Community That Feels Like Home

Sofia Tribe is more than just a group of people – it’s a family. We are a vibrant and welcoming community that brings together locals, expats, and travelers who want to truly experience Sofia in a meaningful way. Whether you’ve just arrived in the city or have been living here for years, you’ll always find a warm atmosphere where everyone is valued and included.

Through our events, cultural activities, and social gatherings, we connect people from all walks of life. From cozy coffee meetups and city tours to exciting nights out and weekend adventures in nature, we create opportunities for genuine friendships and unforgettable memories. It’s about more than just attending events – it’s about feeling part of something special.

Sofia Tribe is where authentic connections happen. We believe that life is better when shared with others, and we’re here to help you discover the best of Sofia while building meaningful relationships that last a lifetime.
